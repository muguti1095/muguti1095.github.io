What is disappointment?

  • - Not one simple emotion it’s a mix
  • - Opposite of disappointment is hope

    "Philippians 2:11"

How can we overcome disappointment

  • - Trust someone who is constant
  • - Fix your eyes on the one who is constant the one who has called (Jesus)
  • - Trust God
  • - When you focus on the things that are passing by you lose your focus
  • - Bring your pain to Jesus (1 Peter 5:7)
  • - Let Christ be the centre of world and you can overcome disappointment
  • - Dethrone yourself and put Christ first
  • - We need to trust and focus on him he’s the centre and doesn’t change
  • - GOD is the same yesterday today and forever
  • - All his ways are perfect
  • - He’s merciful and gracious
  • - World doesn’t revolve around us
  • - When hope is displaced it leads to disappointment
