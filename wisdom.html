Definition of Human Wisdom

“the ability to think and act using understanding, knowledge, sense and insight”

Definition of Godly Wisdom

“seeing things from God's viewpoint and obeying the principle of scripture” - Charles Stanley

Characteristics of Godly Wisdom

  • Peace loving
  • Gentle
  • Willing to yield to others
  • Full of mercy
  • Shows no favoritism
  • Is always sincere

Why Do We Need Godly Wisdom?

  • To make wise decisions
  • To give others wise advice in their times of need
  • To act as an example to others
  • To become more Christ-like
  • To serve effectively in the Church

How Do We Get Godly Wisdom?

  • Godly wisdom can't be attained without first pursuing a relationship with the Lord
  • It is given as a response to a genuine prayerful request to Him
  • It grows by getting to know the Lord through reading and studying God's word and listening to what He is telling us
  • Spend time with others who are Godly - Proverbs 13:20 "Walk with the wise and become wise; associate with fools and get in trouble."

Key Points

  • There is a difference between knowledge and wisdom
  • Godly wisdom will manifest itself in our day-to-day lives
  • Godly wisdom will aid us in our decision making, advice giving, etc.
  • To receive Godly wisdom, we must ask for it, spend time with God, and others who are Godly
